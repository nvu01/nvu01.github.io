## The Curious Analyst

I'm a data analyst who loves digging into messy data, asking a million "why's," and figuring out how to turn complex data into clear insights. While my technical foundation lies in Python, SQL, and data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, what truly sets me apart is how I think critically, creatively, and always looking for ways to make things better.

In just one year, I completed a full 4-year bachelor’s degree in Data Analytics from [Western Governors University](https://www.wgu.edu/). No, I didn’t time travel! I just really like learning, figuring things out fast, and getting stuff done.
